About Aqualogic

Human skin hydration influences both health and performance. Our solution leverages the fact that water in the skin contains electrolytes (affecting resistivity) and near-infrared absorption bands (affecting light transmission).

Technology

  • Electrical Sensing: Ohmmeter to measure resistivity via skin electrolytes.
  • Optical Sensing: Laser + photodetector to capture near-infrared absorption bands.
  • AI Integration: Mobile app processes sensor data to estimate hydration more accurately.
